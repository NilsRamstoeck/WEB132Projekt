About Me

For those who don't know me, please let me introduce myself. My name is Grogu, also known as Baby Yoda and for those who watched "the Mandalorian" known as "The Child".
I was born 41 years BBY (Before the Battle of Yavin) on Planet Dagobah and identify as Yoda Species. I am 5.000 years old. My favorite foods are frogs and fav color green.
I stand at only 1.1ft (0.42m). But don't underestimate me, don't let my cute and innocent face with tiny body fool you, because I could take you down in blink of an eye.

Together with my best friend Mando who carries me most of the time, we have visited many awesome planets. We've been friends for over 10 years and met on the planet Aevala-7. In 2019 we were lucky to be selected and play major roles in a TV Show called "The Mandalorian". It's based on a true story how Mando and I met and what we've been through. The Show got praised by many people. One day, we hope to win a Grammy for the best acting. Nevertheless, we're really proud of the show and our achievements so far. You can watch the trailer here ->
