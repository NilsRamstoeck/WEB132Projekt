Shipping and Tracking

Orders above 39.99€ have free shipping + tracking number.

Orders below 39.99€ have a shipping fee of 5€.

When you purchase an item from our store, it will take 24 hours for your order to be shipped. You will receive an e-mail which will allow you to easily track your package with the tracking number we have provided.

Return Policy

If you're not happy with the item you've received, it arrived damaged or maybe piece of clothing you ordered doesn't fit you.. We have provided a free return label.

Order can be returned within 10 days of item arriving at your door step.

Refund

Typically the refund takes 1-3 business days to be reflicted on your account after it has arrived at our warehouse. Please be patient.
